Data retention policy

This application stores account data and hour registration data for active users. Accounts that have not logged in for {{ retention_days }} days are automatically removed, including related hour entries and security tokens.

Where possible, a reminder email is sent {{ warning_days }} days before scheduled deletion.

Deletion is based on the user's last login timestamp. If the account has never been used, the creation date is used instead.

Administrators can run retention cleanup manually from the admin panel, and the application also performs periodic retention checks automatically during normal use.
